Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order by going to the "My Orders" page and clicking on "Track Order" next to your order. You'll see real-time updates on your delivery status.
What is the return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Some products like electronics may have different return windows.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary based on your location and the product. Most orders are delivered within 2-5 business days. You can see the estimated delivery date on the product page and during checkout.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept various payment methods including UPI, Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, Wallets, and Cash on Delivery (for select locations). All payments are secured with 256-bit encryption.
How do I cancel my order?
You can cancel your order from the "My Orders" page before it is shipped. Once shipped, you cannot cancel but can refuse delivery or initiate a return after delivery.
Do you offer cash on delivery?
Yes, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for orders up to ₹50,000 in select locations. A convenience fee of ₹50 may apply for COD orders.
